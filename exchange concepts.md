# 🏰 Baahubali Characters Representing ALL Exchange Online Concepts

A **Baahubali-themed visualization of Microsoft Exchange Online**, mapping the entire email ecosystem to iconic characters and elements from the Baahubali universe.

This **full universe mapping** covers:
- Mail flow
- Security
- Compliance
- Hybrid
- Permissions
- Audit & monitoring
- Devices
- Migration
- Groups
- Connectors
- Archiving
- External threats
